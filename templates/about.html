About AI Ocean Data Platform

The AI Ocean Data Platform is designed to collect, analyze, and visualize oceanographic and fisheries data using Artificial Intelligence. The platform helps researchers, marine scientists, and environmental organizations monitor marine ecosystems and make data-driven decisions for ocean conservation.


Our Mission

Our mission is to support sustainable ocean management by providing a centralized platform where marine data can be collected and analyzed. By using AI technologies, we aim to identify environmental patterns, predict ocean conditions, and improve marine biodiversity protection.


Objectives

• Collect oceanographic data such as water temperature, species observations, and location.
• Use AI algorithms to analyze patterns in marine ecosystems.
• Support fisheries management and sustainable seafood practices.
• Help scientists track climate change impacts on oceans.
• Provide an easy-to-use platform for data submission and monitoring.


Key Features

• AI-powered ocean data analysis
• Marine biodiversity monitoring
• Ocean temperature and species tracking
• Data submission by researchers and users
• Real-time insights for environmental monitoring


Technology Used

Frontend: HTML, CSS
Backend: Python / Flask
Database: MySQL / SQLite
AI Models: Machine Learning algorithms for environmental data analysis


Impact

By combining oceanographic data with artificial intelligence, this platform can help governments, researchers, and environmental organizations better understand ocean ecosystems. This leads to improved conservation strategies, sustainable fishing practices, and better protection of marine biodiversity.
